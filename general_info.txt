Today, we are going to learn about the development of women's participation the Olympics.

Women began to take part in the Olympics of 1900, after its first year in 1896.
Since then, women's participation in the games has been increasing continuously.
In the 2020 Tokyo Olympic games, women made up of almost 49% of all participants.
The upcoming 2024 Paris Olympics are set to be the first Olympics
with an equal number of male and female athletes, showcasing
the International Olympic Committee's commitment to gender equality in sports
